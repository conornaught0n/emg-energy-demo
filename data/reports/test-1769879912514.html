Field Observations

Note 1: Property assessment beginning at 123 Oak Avenue. Two storey detached house approximately 165 square meters. Building appears to be well maintained with modern construction standards.

Note 2: Blower door test setup complete. Testing at 50 Pascals pressure differential. Weather conditions are good with minimal wind. Temperature 12 degrees Celsius.

Note 3: Air permeability test complete. Reading shows 3.8 cubic meters per hour per square meter at 50 Pascals. This is well within Part L requirements of maximum 5.0.

Note 4: Wall U-value measurements taken using thermal camera. Front wall measures 0.27, side walls 0.29, rear wall 0.28 watts per square meter kelvin. All walls meeting Part L standards.

Note 5: Roof insulation inspection complete. Insulation depth measured at 350 millimeters of mineral wool. U-value calculated at 0.16 watts per square meter kelvin. Excellent thermal performance.

Recommendations

Property achieves full compliance. No immediate remedial works required. Recommend continued maintenance of seals around windows and doors. Consider periodic re-testing in 5-10 years to verify continued performance.
