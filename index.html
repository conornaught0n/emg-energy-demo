üéØ Key Features

AI-Powered: OpenAI Whisper for voice transcription + GPT-4o Vision for handwriting OCR

Compliance: Automatic checking against Irish Building Regulations Part L (TGD L 2022)

Professional Output: EMG-branded PDF reports with charts, compliance analysis, and recommendations

Time Savings: Reduces report creation from 2-3 hours to 15-20 minutes (85% reduction)

üí° How It Works

1. Field Capture: Operators use mobile app to record voice notes and take photos of equipment screens and handwritten clipboards
2. AI Processing: System automatically transcribes voice (Whisper) and extracts data from photos (GPT-4o Vision)
3. Manager Review: Manager reviews AI-processed data, edits if needed, and approves
4. Report Generation: System generates professional PDF with EMG branding, compliance analysis, and improvement recommendations
